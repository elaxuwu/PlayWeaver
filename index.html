How it Works

One clean flow from prompt to playable prototype.

1. Brainstorm with AI.

Chat with high-speed models on Featherless.ai to define mechanics, characters, and settings in natural language.

2. Automatic Visual Design Doc.

Watch as PlayWeaver and Miro instantly generate your Game Design Document whiteboard with sticky notes.

FAQ

Designed to feel simple from the very first prompt.

Do I need a game engine?

No. The prototype output is framed as a single-file HTML5 Canvas game, so the page can communicate speed and accessibility immediately.

What happens after I prompt?

PlayWeaver brainstorms with AI, generates a visual design doc in Miro, and prepares a playable prototype concept from the same idea.

Who is this for?

Founders, designers, game jam teams, and anyone who wants a believable prototype before investing in a full production pipeline.
